Why Become Certified?

Becoming a Certified Internal Auditor® (CIA®) is the optimum way to communicate knowledge, skills, and competencies to effectively carry out professional responsibilities for any internal audit, anywhere in the world.

In the simplest terms...

Internal auditors seek the Certified Internal Auditor (CIA®) certification to:

  • Enhance credibility and respect
  • Increase advancement and earning potential *
  • Sharpen skills and proficiencies *
  • Demonstrate understanding and commitment

*Disclaimer: The information is sought from IIA website and is provided on an "as is" basis. IIA is the owner of information and reserves all rights pertaining to it. For details, refer to the IIA website - https://www.theiia.org/en/certifications/cia/

According to the IIA’s Internal Audit Compensation study 2017 (based on U.S. responses), individuals holding the CIA Certification earn 51% more than those without the certification*.
